About Zenloop
Zenloop is a leading Customer Experience Management platform that helps businesses collect, analyze, and act on customer feedback at scale. Founded in 2016, we serve over 500 enterprise clients across Europe and North America.

Our Mission
To empower businesses to create exceptional customer experiences through intelligent feedback management and actionable insights.
