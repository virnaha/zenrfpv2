REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL
Customer Experience Platform Selection

OVERVIEW:
Our organization is seeking a comprehensive customer experience management platform to replace our current survey tools and improve our customer feedback programs. We need a solution that can handle multi-channel feedback collection, real-time analytics, and automated response workflows.

KEY REQUIREMENTS:

1. Survey Capabilities
- Net Promoter Score (NPS) surveys with industry benchmarking
- Customer Satisfaction (CSAT) measurement across touchpoints
- Customer Effort Score (CES) tracking and optimization
- Custom survey design and branding options

2. Analytics & Reporting
- Real-time dashboard and reporting capabilities
- Advanced segmentation and filtering options
- Predictive analytics and trend identification
- Executive-level reporting and insights

3. Integration & Technical Requirements
- API access for data integration with CRM systems
- Single sign-on (SSO) authentication support
- GDPR compliance and data security measures
- Cloud-based deployment with 99.9% uptime SLA

4. Automation & Workflow
- Automated follow-up workflows for detractors
- Closed-loop feedback management
- Alert notifications for critical feedback
- Case management capabilities

5. Vendor Qualifications
- Proven track record with enterprise clients
- Customer success and support programs
- Implementation timeline of 60 days or less
- Competitive pricing with clear ROI metrics

EVALUATION CRITERIA:
- Technical capabilities (40%)
- Vendor experience and references (25%)
- Implementation approach and timeline (20%)
- Total cost of ownership (15%)

TIMELINE:
- RFP responses due: October 15, 2024
- Vendor presentations: October 20-25, 2024
- Final decision: November 1, 2024
- Implementation start: December 1, 2024

Please provide detailed responses addressing all requirements and include relevant case studies demonstrating successful implementations in similar organizations.
